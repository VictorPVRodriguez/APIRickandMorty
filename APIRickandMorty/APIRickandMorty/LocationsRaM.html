Anatomy Park

Anatomy Park was a location that was featured in the episode of the same name. It was revealed to be an amusement park that was found in the homeless man named Ruben. The two creators of Anatomy Park were Rick and Dr. Xenon Bloom. It was revealed that the main attractions in Anatomy Park were monsterized Diseases. The park was destroyed when Rick took Ruben into space and blew him up.
